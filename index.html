About Me

Medical doctor and independent consultant in health and development with over 25 years’ experience leading public health initiatives across Africa. Passionate about harnessing research, monitoring & evaluation, and human‑capacity building to improve health outcomes. Currently serving as a Monitoring & Evaluation Specialist for regional malaria elimination programs and providing clinical services at a private wellness clinic in Ondangwa, Namibia.

Academic credentials include an MBBS from the University of Ibadan, an MSc in Epidemiology (LSHTM), an MBA, and a PhD in Public Health. Member of the Global Health Council and chair of the Ondangwa Health Professionals Journal Club.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research – Productivity Bootcamp 2025 Q2

This hands‑on 8‑week program equips professionals to deploy leading AI tools without coding, elevating productivity and innovation in research, creative, and business contexts. Key skills gained:

  • Create professional websites, documents, and presentations with AI assistance
  • Conduct literature reviews, analyze data, and build dashboards with intuitive AI tools
  • Design custom chatbots for domain‑specific questions
  • Set up automated workflows that operate around the clock
  • Generate and edit images, videos, and design materials using AI
  • Integrate practical AI solutions into everyday projects

By course completion, participants harness AI to save time, produce higher quality work, and solve real‑world challenges.
